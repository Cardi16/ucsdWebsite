Love Live! Nijigasaki High School Idol Club

So, one thing I really like talking about for some reason is Love Live! and, of my favorite series in Love Live!, Nijigasaki High School Idol Club, especially. To give a brief introduction, Love Live is a series about girls that are idols, but in school, so they're called school idols (wow how original). For 3/5 of the groups in the series, their main goal is to get to and win the in-universe Love Live competition, for school idols, a very lofty goal. Along the way, the series is primarily known for its interpersonal relationships between the girls as they go on their journey to achieve the dreams, a la K-ON's 'cute girls doing cute things'-style.

The aforementioned Nijigasaki High School Idol Club, lined up on a rooftop.

Anyways, these girls are different because, unlike the 4/5 other groups in Love Live, they're not trying to get into Love Live.
